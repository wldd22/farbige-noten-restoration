# Graphic Types

> For identifier formats, see [Coding & Identifier Standards](docs/coding-standards.md)

Each graphic receives **exactly one primary type** based on its function in the original publication.

---

## PL - Plate

Full-page illustrative material made with chromolithography.

---

## OR - Ornament

Decorative typographic elements without semantic content.

---

# TA - Table

Inline information in the form of a grid.

---

## DI - Diagram

Inline graphics conveying conceptual or instructional information.

---

## IN - Initial

Decorative, enlarged initial letters marking the start of a new section.
