# Page Types

> For identifier formats, see [Coding & Identifier Standards](docs/coding-standards.md)

Each page receives **exactly one primary type** based on its function in the original publication.

---

## TP - Title Page

Pages whose primary function is to present the workâ€™s identity: title, author, publisher, place, and date.

---

## TC - Table of Contents

Pages intended for lookup rather than linear reading.

---

## PR - Preface

Contextual or explanatory pages preceding the main text.

---

## BT - Body Text

Pages forming the core content.

---

## PL - Plate Page

Pages whose primary content is a plate or full-page illustration, usually produced with chromolithography.

---

## CO - Colophon Page

Pages used only to give information about the publisher.

---

## SL - Series List

Pages used only to list other works in the same series.

---

## NO - Author's note

Additional material added by the author, generally regarding the work.

---
